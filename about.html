About Your Safety Partners

Our workplace safety specialists are led by John Tregambe, with over 100 years of combined experience in safety solution design and implementation for the private sector.

John has extensive experience with large-scale safety systems — cutting his teeth at BP, where he led Health, Safety & Environment Learning and Development across 5,000+ employees and contractors in Australia, New Zealand and the South-West Pacific.

This equips us to quickly analyse business safety needs and formulate solutions that maintain compliance with all workplace safety regulations.

Our philosophy

We don't believe safety is just about compliance. It's central to what we do — but we see it as an opportunity to improve your business processes, finding better and safer ways to get the job done that save time and money while reducing injuries and incidents.

We formulate solutions that fit your process rather than the other way around — so safe practices simply become part of how work gets done, and can be sustained with far less effort.
