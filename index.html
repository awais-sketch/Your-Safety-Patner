§ 07 · Common questions

The things operations managers ask us first.

How is the 50% off funded? What's the catch?

There isn't one. We're offering 50 discounted reviews this month because we're launching Your Safety Portal into the Australian market and want teams inside the system quickly. Same consultants, same review, half the price, for the first fifty businesses to sign on.

We already have documentation. Do we still need this?

Yes, that's exactly who it's built for. If your documents already exist, we review them against current AS/NZS standards, flag what's out-of-date or missing, and load everything into the portal so your team can actually find things when an auditor asks. Most teams underestimate the gap between "we have docs" and "our docs are audit-ready."

What happens after the 14 days?

You keep the gap report and 30-day action plan. If you want to continue using Your Safety Portal past day 14, we'll share ongoing plans, but there's no auto-renew, no forced upgrade, no lock-in. The review outputs are yours to keep either way.

What industries do you work in?

Construction, manufacturing, warehousing, industrial, transport, waste management, and agriculture. Anywhere Australian WHS obligations apply to documented records, contractor management, hazard tracking, and audit readiness.

Who does the review: a real consultant or an AI?

A real, WHS-certified consultant. Your Safety Portal handles the storage, versioning, and alerts, but the review itself is done by a human who reads your policies, spots the gaps, and flags what needs to happen first.

How fast can we start?

Within one business day of you submitting the request form. A consultant will reach out to confirm scope, kick off the portal, and start the review. Full review turnaround is 14 days from record share.
