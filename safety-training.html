Training that fits your workplace

We can help by developing training to suit the needs of your organisation, or you can choose from our range of compliance-based courses. Our workplace health and safety training services cover:

  • Workplace safety training needs analysis
  • Development of safety training programs to suit different job roles
  • Setting up custom learning management systems (training database)
  • Face-to-face training development and delivery
  • Development and deployment of online learning

Online learning

Your Safety Partners are specialists in the development of online learning in workplace health and safety. We combine our safety expertise with strong creative and technical ability to develop content that can be deployed across multiple sites simultaneously, and edited quickly when procedural or legislative updates are required.

Our experienced instructional designers work with you to build fully interactive courses that engage your staff and deliver the competency you require. We convert paper-based materials into interactive courses featuring your own images, video and audio. You fully own the course and can launch it from your own LMS, or use ours.

Work Instructions

We have developed hundreds of Work Instructions across a variety of industries, providing step-by-step guidance on how to safely perform a particular job. We observe and document each step pictorially, and at the same time conduct a risk assessment to identify hazards, which are then mitigated in the instruction.

Each includes a competency assessment signed off by both learner and trainer, which can be uploaded as evidence of competency. If a worker is injured in the workplace, authorities will request evidence that the worker was trained, so Work Instructions are a critical part of any safety system.

What are the legal requirements for safety training in Australia?

Under Australian law, employers are obligated to provide adequate health and safety training to their workers, including workplace inductions, hazard identification and management, and knowledge of safe work practices. Training must be tailored to the specific roles and risks in your workplace, with evidence documented and regularly updated to remain compliant.

What types of safety training are available?

We deliver role-specific inductions, compliance-based courses, face-to-face training, and fully interactive online learning, along with custom Work Instructions and competency assessments, all tailored to your operations.

Why is safety training important for businesses?

Effective training reduces workplace injuries, keeps you compliant, and provides documented evidence of competency, protecting both your people and your business if an incident is ever investigated.

How can Your Safety Partners assist with safety training?

We assess your training needs, develop programs and courses to suit your roles, set up learning management systems, and deliver both face-to-face and online learning, so your team is competent and your records are audit-ready.
