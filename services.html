Our Services

Practical, end-to-end workplace health & safety consulting, Australia-wide. We assess your workplace, clarify your obligations, and support implementation, without unnecessary complexity.

Service 01

Traffic Management

Detailed traffic management planning so pedestrians stay safe around vehicles and mobile plant at all times. We map how people, vehicles and equipment interact on your site, then design practical controls, signage and pedestrian exclusion zones that actually work in your environment.

Speak to us

Service 02

Safety Training

Tailored WHS training that builds competency, meets compliance requirements and reduces risk across every role, from site inductions to role-specific competencies. We develop training to suit your organisation, or draw on a range of compliance-based courses.

Speak to us

Service 03

Machine Safety

Assessment of machinery risks, guarding requirements and compliance with Australian standards to prevent serious injury. We review guarding, isolation and safe operating procedures so your plant runs safely and stays compliant.

Speak to us

Service 04

Plant & Equipment

Inspection, risk assessment and compliance review of plant, equipment and operational systems to ensure safe use. We identify hazards, verify controls and help you maintain safe, well-documented equipment over its life.

Speak to us

Service 05

Job Safety Analysis

Structured task analysis to identify hazards, assess risks and implement practical controls for non-routine and high-risk work, giving your teams clear, step-by-step guidance they can actually follow.

Speak to us

Service 06

Psychosocial Health

Identifying and managing psychosocial risks, workload, bullying, role clarity and organisational change, in line with current WHS obligations, so your workplace supports mental as well as physical safety.

Speak to us

Service 07

Confined Spaces

Compliance assessments, entry procedures and risk controls to manage the hazards of confined space work, including atmospheric monitoring and rescue planning for safe entry and exit.

Speak to us

Service 08

Working at Heights

Review of fall-prevention systems, rescue planning and height-safety controls aligned with regulatory requirements, keeping workers protected wherever there is a risk of a fall.

Speak to us

Service 09

Contractor Management

Systems to manage contractor compliance, inductions, documentation and site-specific safety obligations, so everyone on your site meets the same standard, and you can prove it.

Speak to us

Service 10

Standard Operating Procedures

Development and review of clear, practical SOPs that support safe and consistent work practices, written for the people who actually do the work, not just to sit in a folder.

Speak to us

Service 11

Incident Investigations

Independent investigation of workplace incidents to determine root cause, corrective actions and preventive measures, helping you learn from events and stop them recurring.

Speak to us

Service 12

Safety Audits

Comprehensive audits to identify compliance gaps, assess risk exposure and provide clear, prioritised implementation guidance, so you know exactly where you stand and what to do next.
